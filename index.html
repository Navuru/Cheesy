    Cilantro-Lime Chicken

    Ingredients

    1. 4 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
    2. Juice of 2 limes
    3. 1/4 c.Add freshly chopped cilantro
    4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
    5. 1/2 tsp. cumin
    6. Pinch crushed red pepper flakes
    7. 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
    8. Kosher salt
    9. Freshly ground black pepper

    Instructions

    1. Whisk together 2 tablespoons oil, lime juice, cilantro, garlic, cumin, and red pepper
      flakes. Add chicken and toss to coat. Let marinate in fridge 30 minutes and up to 2 hours.
    2. When ready to cook, preheat oven to 425Â°. In a large ovenproof skillet over
      medium-high heat, heat remaining 2 tablespoons oil. Season chicken with salt
      and pepper, then add to skillet skin-side down and pour in remaining marinade.
      Sear until skin is golden and crispy, about 6 minutes. Flip and cook 2 minutes more
    3. Transfer skillet to oven and bake until chicken is cooked through, 10 to 12 minutes.
    4. Serve over rice drizzled with pan drippings.
